A collection of my writings, from the day our daughter Olivia passed away until the day our son Ben was born 14 months later.

These were the darkest days of my life... but light shines in the dark. The lessons I learned during these days, while painful, will serve me the rest of my life. I hope they will serve you, too.

If I were to sum up this book in only a few lines, it would be this:

It's not a marathon.
You don't have to be strong.
It's okay to fall apart.
You can only be where you are.
You don't have to control.
The waves will come, and they will go.
It's not a marathon .

I hope, as you read this book, you begin to physically feel the truth of the above words, as hope and healing find their way in to your heart.
‍
—Nathan Peterson
