Dance Again: grief is healing

A collection of my writings, from the day our daughter Olivia passed away until the day our son Ben was born 14 months later.
In these pages I share learning which applies to so many different kinds of grief and feelings of loss, big and small.
Download a sample chapter
"Dance Again shaped my thinking to more clearly see that the path to true humanness requires the courage of authenticity. A book that should be read."
—Dr. Larry Crabb
These were the darkest days of my life... but light shines in the dark. The lessons I learned during these days, while painful, will serve me the rest of my life. I hope they will serve you, too. ‍
