BOOKS ABOUT GRIEF AND BOOKS ABOUT HEALING

SO AM I

This book was written during the life of our daughter Olivia who was born with a condition called Trisomy 18. She was given hours to live, but ended up living 14 miraculous months.

This is a book for grieving parents or anyone who has struggled to be present in the midst of grief or uncertainty or fear.

I have also written a collection of songs for grieving parents — songs about being present in the midst of uncertainty — called So Am I.

DANCE AGAIN

This book was written during the time of grieving after the loss of our daughter, Olivia.

This is a book for parents who have lost a child or anyone who grieves the loss of something.

I have also written a collection of songs for parents who have lost a child — songs for comfort — called Dance Again.
