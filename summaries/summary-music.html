SONGS ABOUT LIFE

SO AM I

This album was written during the life of our beautiful daughter Olivia. Olivia was diagnosed with Trisomy 18 and given a very low chance of survival. We were given the gift of 14 months with her, during which time our perception of life and living was dramatically changed.

So Am I is a collection of songs for grieving parents, parents who have lost a child, or anyone who is dealing with loss of something dear to them.

So Am I is a collection of songs about being present, having courage to be here and now.

So Am I is a collection of acoustic songs about life and death — songs about life, living, and letting go, songs about grief, songs about loss, songs for comfort, songs about losing a loved one.

I have also written a book for grieving parents called So Am I: 14 Months of Live, Living, and Letting Go.

DANCE AGAIN

This album was written during the months and years of grief after the passing of our child.

Dance Again is a collection of sad songs and hopeful songs for bereaved parents, parents who have lost a child, or anyone who is grieving. These are meaningful songs for comfort and songs of healing. These are song about losing someone, songs for feeling sad, songs for feeling angry, songs for feeling.

I have also written a book for grieving parents called Dance Again: Grief is Healing.
