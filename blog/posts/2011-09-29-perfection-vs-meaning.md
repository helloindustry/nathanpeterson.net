---
date: 2011-09-29
title: "Perfection vs Meaning"
---

No one is looking for perfect art. Just that phrase, "perfect art", sounds
stupid. It's cold and boring. Sterile.

Aren't we much more concerned with *meaning* than perfection? If your art has
meaning *and* perfection, great. But no matter how perfect your execution is,
if you play me a song that doesn't say something (musically or lyrically or
some other way), I'll never listen to it again. And worse, it won't help me.

__Meaning is art.__ Perfection is just something to distract attention from
the fact that we don't actually have anything to say.
