date: 2011-09-29
title: "Blog Post: Perfection vs Meaning"
---
<p>No one is looking for perfect art. Just that phrase, "perfect art", sounds stupid. It's cold and boring. Sterile.</p>
<p>Aren't we much more concerned with <em>meaning</em> than perfection? If your art has meaning <em>and</em> perfection, great. But no matter how perfect your execution is, if you play me a song that doesn't say something (musically or lyrically or some other way), I'll never listen to it again. And worse, it won't help me.</p>
<p><strong>Meaning is art. </strong>Perfection is just something to distract attention from the fact that we don't actually have anything to say.</p>
