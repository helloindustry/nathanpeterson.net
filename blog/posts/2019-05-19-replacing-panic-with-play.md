title: "Replacing panic with play"
---
This is one of
the most vivid (to me) pictures of what it is to be restored—whole. It may not
be something we can decide to do on command ("I'm going to play instead of
panic right now"), but there are things we can do to restore the foundation
needed for play: trust. confidence. rest. Are there ways today that I can
cultivate my own trust, confidence, rest? Are there habits I am allowing which
do the opposite?
