---
date: 2014-04-03
title: "Failure doesn't lead to the things we're afraid
of"
---

Failure doesn’t lead to the things we’re afraid of. How we *deal* with failure
determines if those things happen. But how we deal with failure also
determines if we *learn something, get up, and keep our resolve to move
forward, now with an even clearer vision.*

Failure is the door to our biggest fears… and our greatest potential.

“Nothing is terrible except fear itself.” (Sir Francis Bacon)
