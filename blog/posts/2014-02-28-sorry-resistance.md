date: 2014-02-28
title: "Sorry, Resistance"
---

I've observed that on weeks when I'm releasing something to the public, my
internal battles are much more intense. Things seem impossible. I'm angry for
no reason. I fight with my wife. I'm distant from my kids.

Release week = difficult week. I think that's the resistance Stephen
Pressfield﻿ talks about.

My strategy so far: __suck it up and release the thing__ , apologize to who
I've hurt, learn from my mistakes for next time.

What I will not do is give up.
