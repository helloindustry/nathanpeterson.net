<p>A friend of mine often asks me to look for "a way forward which is nutritive" to me. A way to tell if something feels "nutritive" is if, when you envision it, it feels good in your body &mdash; your body lets you know, even when your mind won't (or can't).</p><p><br></p><p>This is different than my typical black and white thinking that anything helpful to others depletes me, and anything nutritive to me depletes someone else.</p><p><br></p><p>There are ways forward which can be helpful to others *and* nutritive to me. It just takes a little work to find them, and more than work, it takes trust &mdash; learning to trust our intuition, what we hear deep in our center.</p>
