<p>Entire skating rink to me and Heather and the boys. Thanks to Monica and mom for watching the girls so we can feel normal for a little bit. :) </p>

<p>Heather and I were talking this week about the choice we have to either let hard circumstances be the lens which we view every moment through, or to enjoy living and playing even in the midst of hard circumstances - deciding that there is no guarantee things will ever get “better” - accepting reality but not enjoying life any less because of it. We can’t control circumstances, but we can control our posture.</p>

<p>This week has been very difficult with Olivia. Several all-nighters. Some big scares. We’re tired and haven’t been able to connect much. I feel behind with my work. But every moment we have the above choice.</p>

<p>Today we chose to ice skate and eat burgers with the boys. Today was really fun. Today there is nothing wrong. That’s not our circumstance - it’s what we choose.</p>
