Today, when I notice myself reaching for my phone or some other way to avoid a
feeling I just had, I will pause, turn, and face the feeling.

  

I will pause to let myself feel that feeling--not just in my head, but all the
way down to my gut.

  

I will pause to let my body respond to that feeling however it wants--crying,
screaming, laughing, smiling.

  

I will pause to appreciate the kind thing I just did for myself.
