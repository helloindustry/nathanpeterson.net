---
date: 2013-10-30
title: "Why I Work Non-Stop"
---

I'm afraid money won't come in. So I work harder and non-stop. The truth is
that my harder working never results in more money. In fact, it sometimes
scares away business. The truth is that my harder, faster, non-stop working
keeps me busy and distracted from my fear of not having enough money and
failing to provide for my family. I'm solving the wrong problem with my
current reaction.
