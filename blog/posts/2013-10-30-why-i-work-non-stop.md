title: "Blog Post: Why I Work Non-Stop"
---
<p>I&#8217;m afraid money won&#8217;t come in. So I work harder and non-stop. The truth is that my harder working never results in more money. In fact, it sometimes scares away business. The truth is that my harder, faster, non-stop working keeps me busy and distracted from my fear of not having enough money and failing to provide for my family. I&#8217;m solving the wrong problem with my current reaction.</p>
