<p>Dear music community, this is obviously a difficult time for all of us. While we do our part to slow the spread, our community is in dire need of human connection and music and art.</p><p>I&rsquo;m the first to point out the value of sharing a physical space, but while that is not possible there are plenty of other ways to connect through music.</p><p>Many of you are musicians. Almost all of you have the ability to stream video... please do that.</p><p>Don&rsquo;t wait for it to be good. Don&rsquo;t worry about lighting. No edits. Don&rsquo;t polish it. Just go live or hit record and share your Voice with us.</p><p>This is good for you, and also a way for you to support your community, which is crucial right now.</p><p>Let&rsquo;s fill the &ldquo;airwaves&rdquo; with music &mdash; and while we&rsquo;re at it, let&rsquo;s fill our music with ourselves: &ldquo;me, just I am, where I am.&rdquo; This can be delivered in a song you wrote, a cover, or instrumental music &mdash; if you decide you give yourself it will be beautiful and it will help.</p><p>We need your music.</p><p>We need your humanity.</p>
