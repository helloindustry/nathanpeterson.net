date: 2014-02-05
title: "REST"
---
<p>After 34 years of pushing, I&#8217;m trying a new approach: Stop forging ahead and <strong>rest</strong>. Trust. <em>Let go</em>.</p>

<p>This new way of operating has been terrifying for me. For someone who has survived by gaining control of every variable, this approach has triggered a similar internal response to what might happen if I were to jump off a cliff without a rope. In my mind, the only way is down. But in the very back of my mind, there&#8217;s a sense that there may be another way. Beyond all my hopes for changing the world and impacting millions is a deeper hope: to be free. Free from all of the pushing. Free from the need for the world to validate my existence by &#8220;letting me help them&#8221;. Free from the lie I have to live to be in &#8220;ministry&#8221; when in reality I&#8217;m taking much more than I&#8217;m giving. A picture is forming in my mind of a bird being pushed from the nest - and while the only outcome it could have imagined was death, <strong>the reality of flight is just about to change everything</strong>.</p>
