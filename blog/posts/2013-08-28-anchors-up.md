---
date: 2013-08-28
title: "Anchors Up"
---

The wind blows where it pleases. We toss an anchor. We never see where it was
going.

What kind of things might we see if we *let go*. Anchors up. Untethered.
Blowing with the wind. Flying.

__We don't have to wonder.__ We were meant to fly. Anchors up.
