title: "3 things I'm learning:"
---

1. It’s better to walk the right path than an easy one.

2. Real life is hard, but it’s still better than fantasy, because it’s
*real*.

3. It’s better to pursue integrity* than popularity. And both is never an
option.

*By integrity, I mean doing what you know is best, no matter what.
