Is it possible that it is not my job to find my voice?

  

Is it possible that my voice is emerging, on its own, in its time... that my
job is only to trust?

Instead of pushing yourself today, sense the pace the Current is going.
Cooperate with it--not in front, not behind. Appreciate this part of the
journey, where you are today.

If it hurts, then it hurts. If it feels good, that's fine too. Neither will
last. Because tomorrow is a new day.

Today, be here. You will never be here again.
