We should start the week checking in on what&#x27;s going on inside us rather than what&#x27;s going on around us.

Instead of checking email and Facebook first-thing, we should become quiet, open a journal, and ask some questions:
- what am I excited about right now?
- what am I afraid of right now?
- what is essential?
- what can I let go of?
