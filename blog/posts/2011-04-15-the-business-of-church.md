<div class='posterous_autopost'><p>  <p>The Business of Church is weird. Doesn't it feel a little bit like "who asked us to do this?" Like a lot of self-imposed stress and crisis? I know, God asked us to do this. But is there a slight possibility that what it's become is, at least partially, bloated?<p />I think the American Church may be experiencing some serious feature bloat.<p />Don't get me wrong - I think the quality of things coming out of the church is still way to low. It's half-baked and usually a copy of something done much better by someone else. But features and quality are two *very* different things.</p>  </p></div>
