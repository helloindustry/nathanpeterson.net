---
date: 2013-08-28
title: "To Love Generously"
---

__" I don't need this to work for me."__ Now I'm free to *give*. __What I give
is no longer a bargaining chip.  __Now I'm free to love generously.

Deep down we all want to give and love like this. But we're surviving just
like the next guy. "I need you to affirm who I am." We say this to customers,
bosses, fans, friends, spouses, our children. __This is the largest battle
inside us - to live selfishly, or love generously.__ To give figs, or to
withhold them.

__Until we stop requiring everyone and everything around us to affirm our
identity, we 're never free to give and love generously__. And to give and
love generously *is* who we really are.
