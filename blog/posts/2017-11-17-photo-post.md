My newest single, 'Is It a Sin?' is available everywhere today: http://smarturl.it/isitasin

This is a hard song, but I think it echos the sentiments of many of us at one point or another during our life. I believe it is so SO important to let moments like these exist as they are; to not force ourselves to move quickly through them; to not contort ourselves into a smile, saying the right words out loud while we silence what is true inside. No. Real is better. In every given moment, you can only be where you are. If this is too much, I understand if you don't listen. For those of you who do, I'd love to hear your impressions after listening. ❤️
