This may be my new favorite brewer. It's the beehouse. I waited until trying
just about every other brewer because of it's reputation as the easiest one to
use. Not sure why I did that - I guess I just like to do things the hard way.
Using [Andy Sprenger's method](http://sprudge.com/andy-sprenger-brewers-cup-
king-the-exclusive-interview.html), I'm getting some of the best cups I've
ever brewed with this thing!
