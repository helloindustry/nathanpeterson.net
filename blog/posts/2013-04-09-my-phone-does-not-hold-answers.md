title: "My Phone Does Not Hold Answers"
---
<p>A lesson I'm currently learning: <strong>My phone does not hold answers. Neither does my laptop.</strong> The direction I'm looking for is most likely inside of me already, or will be whispered to me when I'm quiet.</p>
<p>Therefore, if I don't know what to do, I should *not* go to my phone or laptop. I should go to a quiet place or open my journal and start writing.</p>
