date: 2013-04-09
title: "My Phone Does Not Hold Answers"
---

A lesson I'm currently learning: __My phone does not hold answers. Neither
does my laptop.__ The direction I'm looking for is most likely inside of me
already, or will be whispered to me when I'm quiet.

Therefore, if I don't know what to do, I should *not* go to my phone or
laptop. I should go to a quiet place or open my journal and start writing.
