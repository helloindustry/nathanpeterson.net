Out Today!! Dance Again: Grief is Healing 🌊

This is a follow-up to my previous book, So Am I. Dance Again is about grief and healing.

When Olivia died two years ago and we began this grief journey, I had no idea what we were walking into. My understanding of grief—and of life—has once again been changed, and I have the moments of these past two years to thank. One thing I’ve learned is that without grieving there can be no healing—that in fact, grief *is* healing. In that way, this book is not only for those who have lost someone or something—it is for any of us who are in need of healing. I hope this book helps brings that healing to you. ❤️

Dance Again is available today on Amazon and Apple, and there are a limited supply of signed copies available directly from my website.
