# Sundays

There is no "going to Church" when you *are* the Church. Church happens best
today by *being*, not by going.

  

Going to a church service can be good, but it is not a substitute for our
responsibility today to *be*.

  

Sundays are set aside as a day of rest. Rest is the act of coming back to
where we are, of slowing/stopping the going, and of cultivating the immense
amount of trust (faith) it takes to let go of our doing and simply to be--to
be where we are, and who we are.
