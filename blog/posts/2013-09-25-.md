I submit to the way things work.  
I choose to trust the process.  
I don't need to rush.  
I don't need to prove anything.  
I'm free to love. To give. To sit. To create.  
Things will go where they go.  
I can't control the wind.  
I choose to let it blow.  
I choose to let it go.
