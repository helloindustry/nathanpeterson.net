Our battle isn&#x27;t against discomfort or uncertainty or death. It&#x27;s not against any person or country or the devil. It&#x27;s against fear.

There&#x27;s nothing we can do about those other things - they&#x27;re a part of life. Fear doesn&#x27;t have to be. It&#x27;s a choice. And every time we make that choice we become more likely to make it again.

Cutting a new path is hard, but I believe our lives depend on disciplining ourselves to choose trust over fear.
