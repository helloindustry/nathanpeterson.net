I've always avoided reading
popular books, assuming I'd lose my edge if I read what "the masses" we're
reading. I'm changing my mind about that. Everything we take in is interpreted
by our own unique set of perceptions, values, and experiences. Reading a given
book may move one person to do nothing while it inspires another to change the
world. **A book's impact is only as powerful as the reader's potential to act
on it.**
