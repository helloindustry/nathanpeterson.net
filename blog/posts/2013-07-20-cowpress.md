<p>Made this up this morning. One if the best coffee recipes I&#8217;ve tried in 3yrs. Enjoy!</p>

<p>"Cowpress"</p>

<p>340g water just off boil
24g coarse grind</p>

<p>Use any container you wish for brewing. A French press container without the top can work fine. So can a mason jar.</p>

<p>Pre rinse an aeropress filter and have that ready with another container or 12oz mug.</p>

<p>Pour 100g and swirl so all grounds are wet<br>
Start timer<br>
Pour remaining 240g, stir and cover (I used a plate)<br>
At 2m, stir up grounds sitting on the bottom and re-cover<br>
At 5m, dunk grounds floating in top and skim off anything remaining, then wait 1m<br>
At 6m, carefully pour into rinsed aeropress - don&#8217;t pour very end sludgy stuff<br>
Plunge slowly</p>

<p>You should be left with a very fine, thin cake of shiny sludge in your aeropress, and a very clean, complex cup off coffee in your mug. ;)</p>

<p>Try it and let me know how it works for you!</p>
