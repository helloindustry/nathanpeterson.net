Churches could agree on a "critical mass" which they believe is necessary for them to reach their community. Once critical mass is reached, all excess resources and energy could be shared (given) directly to other churches in order to help them reach their critical mass (potential). They could call themselves a "Critical Mass Church" or a "Big C Church" and they could display all consumed and shared resources publicly on their website to keep themselves accountable.
