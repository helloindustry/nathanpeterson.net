<p>Today, when I notice myself reaching for my phone or some other way to avoid a feeling I just had, I will pause, turn, and face the feeling.</p><p><br></p><p>I will pause to let myself feel that feeling&mdash;not just in my head, but all the way down to my gut.</p><p><br></p><p>I will pause to let my body respond to that feeling however it wants&mdash;crying, screaming, laughing, smiling.</p><p><br></p><p>I will pause to appreciate the kind thing I just did for myself.</p>
