<div class='posterous_autopost'><p>  <p style="margin: 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px; font: 12.0px Helvetica;">How many times have you started a sentence that way?</p>  <p style="margin: 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px; font: 12.0px Helvetica;">&nbsp;</p>  <p style="margin: 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px; font: 12.0px Helvetica;">"Once I get on my feet I'll..."</p>  <p style="margin: 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px; font: 12.0px Helvetica;">"As soon as... then I'll..."</p>  <p style="margin: 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px; font: 12.0px Helvetica;">&nbsp;</p>  <p style="margin: 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px; font: 12.0px Helvetica;">It's a piece of equipment. A loan. Someone's approval. A sure sign from God Almighty. An open door. A few more dollars in the bank. One more book on the subject under your belt. A degree. Some condition.</p>  <p style="margin: 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px; font: 12.0px Helvetica;">&nbsp;</p>  <p style="margin: 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px; font: 12.0px Helvetica;">How would you live your life if someone told you that the condition would never be met - that you'd never "get on your feet"? That you'll never have enough money or time or approval or clarity. Then what?</p>  <p style="margin: 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px; font: 12.0px Helvetica;">&nbsp;</p>  <p style="margin: 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px; font: 12.0px Helvetica;">Are you prepared to let your dream or calling die?</p>  <p style="margin: 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px; font: 12.0px Helvetica;">&nbsp;</p>  <p style="margin: 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px; font: 12.0px Helvetica;">Here's what I think: I think that 99% of the human potential in our world is<em> </em><strong><em>waiting</em></strong>. And I believe it will die that way.</p>  <p style="margin: 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px; font: 12.0px Helvetica;">&nbsp;</p>  <p style="margin: 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px; font: 12.0px Helvetica;">And I don't think unmet conditions have anything to do with it. These conditions we lay out are just a smoke screen meant to hide the real truth - that we're scared. I'll say it plainly here: <strong>People don't act on their greatest passions and callings because they're afraid</strong>. What if I fail? Or even, what if I succeed?! Then what?</p>  <p style="margin: 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px; font: 12.0px Helvetica;">&nbsp;</p>  <p style="margin: 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px; font: 12.0px Helvetica;">It's much safer to keep our calling on the horizon. Then it's always in sight. It's always possible. It never dies. But let's be honest: It was never alive to begin with, was it?</p>  <p style="margin: 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px; font: 12.0px Helvetica;">&nbsp;</p>  <p style="margin: 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px; font: 12.0px Helvetica;">If you're waiting to get on your feet, you can start by getting off of your butt. ;)</p>  </p></div>
