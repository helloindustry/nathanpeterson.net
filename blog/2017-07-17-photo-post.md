Quiet first. Then doing.

It’s the hardest to keep it in that order and not the other way around. Especially right now. Especially here. So, always.

In a major mind-shift and in rest is your freedom. In quiet and in trust is your strength. But you would have none of it - you say “we will ride on swift horses”. Therefore your pursuers will be swift.
