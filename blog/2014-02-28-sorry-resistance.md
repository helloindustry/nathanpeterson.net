<p>I&#8217;ve observed that on weeks when I&#8217;m releasing something to the public, my internal battles are much more intense. Things seem impossible. I&#8217;m angry for no reason. I fight with my wife. I&#8217;m distant from my kids.</p>

<p>Release week = difficult week. I think that&#8217;s the resistance Stephen  Pressfield﻿ talks about.</p>

<p>My strategy so far: <strong>suck it up and release the thing</strong>, apologize to who I&#8217;ve hurt, learn from my mistakes for next time.</p>

<p>What I will not do is give up.</p>
