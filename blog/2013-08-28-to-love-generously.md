title: "Blog Post: To Love Generously"
---
<p><strong>&ldquo;I don&rsquo;t need this to work for me.&rdquo;</strong> Now I&rsquo;m free to <em>give</em>. <strong>What I give is no longer a bargaining chip.&nbsp;</strong>Now I&rsquo;m free to love generously.</p>
<p>Deep down we all want to give and love like this. But we&rsquo;re surviving just like the next guy. &ldquo;I need you to affirm who I am.&rdquo; We say this to customers, bosses, fans, friends, spouses, our children. <strong>This is the largest battle inside us - to live selfishly, or love generously.</strong> To give figs, or to withhold them.</p>
<p><strong>Until we stop requiring everyone and everything around us to affirm our identity, we&rsquo;re never free to give and love generously</strong>. And to give and love generously <em>is</em> who we really are.</p>
