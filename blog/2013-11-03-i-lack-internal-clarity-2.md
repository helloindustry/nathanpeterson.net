<p>Cloudiness. This may be one of my biggest problems. Not lack of energy or capacity or even courage. But a lack of <em>clarity</em>. Particularly clarity in what is happening inside my self. It&#8217;s a crowded room in there. Lots of activity, voices, sentences, ideas - but combined, it&#8217;s just a lot of noise and leads to nowhere. Somewhere in there is my voice. Somewhere in there, the quietest voice, is God&#8217;s whisper.</p>

<p>My priority should not be on listening harder. <em>My priority should be on silencing the room.</em></p>

<p>I know the world is not set up for this. My phone, internet, Twitter&#8230; It&#8217;s like we&#8217;re trying to avoid silence at all costs. Even in nature I&#8217;m tying myself up in knots. Or a straight jacket. <em>We&#8217;re being rendered useless.</em> And we&#8217;ll be in line again at 6AM to buy the next iPhone the day it comes out - like it&#8217;s a privilege to be continuously shut down and confused like this.</p>

<p>Would you agree that something is off here?</p>
