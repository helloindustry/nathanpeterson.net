<h1>Sundays</h1><p>There is no &ldquo;going to Church&rdquo; when you *are* the Church. Church happens best today by *being*, not by going.</p><p><br></p><p>Going to a church service can be good, but it is not a substitute for our responsibility today to *be*.</p><p><br></p><p>Sundays are set aside as a day of rest. Rest is the act of coming back to where we are, of slowing/stopping the going, and of cultivating the immense amount of trust (faith) it takes to let go of our doing and simply to be&mdash;to be where we are, and who we are.</p>
