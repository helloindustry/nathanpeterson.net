I&#x27;ve always avoided reading popular books, assuming I&#x27;d lose my edge if I read what &quot;the masses&quot; we&#x27;re reading. I&#x27;m changing my mind about that. Everything we take in is interpreted by our own unique set of perceptions, values, and experiences. Reading a given book may move one person to do nothing while it inspires another to change the world. **A book&#x27;s impact is only as powerful as the reader&#x27;s potential to act on it.**
